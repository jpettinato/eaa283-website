By car

Free parking is available on-site. The field is a short drive from downtown Honesdale, off the Owego Turnpike.

Flying in

Cherry Ridge (N30) welcomes visiting aircraft. Check current field conditions and the AWOS before you depart.

Monthly chapter meetings plus Thursday Koala build nights. See the events calendar for dates.
