Who we are

A community of aviators, builders, and enthusiasts.

EAA Chapter 283 is made up of volunteers who keep our mission moving forward. We organize events, support local aviators, and create a welcoming environment for anyone who loves flight.

Together, we inspire learning, celebrate aviation's spirit, and strengthen the bond between pilots and our community.

Young Eagles Flights

A kid's first taste of flight — free.

Our Young Eagles events give kids the chance to experience the magic of flight firsthand. Volunteer pilots offer free introductory flights, answer questions, and share what makes aviation so exciting — a relaxed, family-friendly day built to inspire the next generation of aviators.

Chapter project

We're building the Super Koala.

A two-seat Light Sport kit aircraft from Fisher Flying Products. We meet most Thursdays to build it together — and anyone interested in helping is welcome to join us.
