Our mission

Opening the hangar doors to the next generation.

EAA Chapter 283 brings aviators of all ages together to share knowledge, inspire new pilots, and strengthen our aviation community. We honor our chapter's rich experience while opening the hangar doors to the next generation through mentorship, hands-on learning, and a passion for flight.

We organize events, support local aviators, and create a welcoming environment for anyone who loves aviation — no experience required.

What we stand for

01
